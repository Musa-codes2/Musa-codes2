About Us

Hot Bean Web is a dynamic and forward-thinking tech company dedicated to empowering the next generation of developers. We specialize in providing high-quality training, mentorship, and real-world experience to aspiring web professionals. Our team is made up of industry experts passionate about education, innovation, and creating meaningful opportunities for learners from all backgrounds. Whether you're just starting your coding journey or looking to level up your skills, Hot Bean Web is where potential meets possibility.

We offer a range of hands-on courses and career-focused programs designed to equip students with the practical skills needed in today’s fast-paced tech industry. From front-end development to full-stack solutions, our curriculum stays current with the latest tools and technologies. At Hot Bean Web, we don’t just teach code—we help our students build confidence, portfolios, and lasting connections that open doors to exciting career paths.

Our Journey

Founded in 2020, Hot Bean Web started as a small team of passionate developers and educators who wanted to make tech education more accessible. Over the years, we’ve grown into a vibrant community where learning meets real-world experience. From our first workshop with 10 learners to now supporting hundreds across the UK, our mission has remained the same: empower people to build their future through code. Whether it’s launching new courses, partnering with tech companies, or expanding our mentoring network, we’re proud of how far we’ve come — and even more excited about what’s ahead.
